Job Finding Tips

Here are some good tips to keep in mind when searching for a new job

  • • Networking with friends and coworkers
  • • Bring up your pursuit in conversation
  • • Find a group of people linked to the job you want
  • • meet with a few of your Alumni and acquaintances
  • • Network through social events
  • • Use networking sites, such as LinkedIn
  • • Update your resume frequently
  • • Check job boards

Networking


If you need some help getting a job, it helps to have a few friends, acquaintances, or just nice people who have connections to a job you'd like. The essence of networking is having someone else put in a good word for you with their employer so that you have a better chance of scoring an interview with that employer. There are many ways to get into communities that provide networking opportunities, such as classmates from school, previous co-workers, friends, or even a friend of a friend. Another good way to get in touch with companies and like minded individuals is with networking systems, such as LinkedIn, which can help connect you to like minded individuals and companies that you may have an interest in someone like you. Networking is a worthwhile venture, as it can become a powerful tool in landing the job you want, so never be afraid to get out there in the world!
