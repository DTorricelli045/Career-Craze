About Us

Who are We?

Career Craze is a new non-profit organization that was founded on the 1st of January 2018, by a merry band of experts who aspired to aid the ambitious people of the world in finding the right job for them and assist in obtaining it. They set out to create this website to present top notch tips and never failing assistance. The website provides a broad channel of information for its users, so as to prepare them for writing exceptional applications, having the ideal interview, and creating the most refined of resumes.


Our Mission

Career Craze seeks to educate those with a wealth of advice for earning any job in the most professional manner. Our hope is to do so by providing effective information from across the web and condensing it into an easily accessed website. CareerCraze contains a full collection of informative essays and resourceful links to be used for free by any one of our users to help them score their dream job.


You've got our full support

We hope to see you find success in the great world of work we all participate in every day. Whether we're just contributing to a business, or building an enterprise, everyone's looking to advance their journey for the perfect career, and we would like to help you on your way.

Along with administering advice to the community of job seekers out there, we want to help you find your way about the craze of finding the perfect career. All of our articles were not only written with expert knowledge on the topics they cover, but the resources from which we found that knowledge are listed at the bottom of each page.

In addition to these resources, our administration team is always looking to provide full on support for all your job hunting concerns. Have a question, comment, complaint, concern, or interest? Stop by our Contact page where you can contact one of our administrators, and let us know all about it. You'll get a response via email within 24 hours upon filling out our form. We'd also love to hear your feedback on our website and how we can improve it in the ever changing job market, so please don't be afraid to leave your thoughts with us.
