About Us

Who are We?

Career Craze is a new organization that was founded on the 1st of January 2018, by a merry band of experts who aspired to aid the ambitious people of the world in finding the right job for them and assist in obtaining it. They set out to create this website to present top notch tips and never ailing assistance. The website provides a broad channel of information for its users, so as to prepare them for writing adriot applications, having the ideal interview, and creating the most refined of resumes.


Our Mission

Career Craze seeks to present the most knowledgable of advice for attaining any job one yearns for in the most professional of manners. We hope to do so by providing the knowledgebase this website acts as. It contains a full collection of informative essays and resourceful links for free use by anyone one of our users.


You've got our full support

We hope to see you find success in the great world of work we all participate in nearly every day. Whether we're just contributing to a business, or building an enterprise, everyone's looking to endeavour on their journey for the perfect career. We want to help you on your way

Along with administering advice to the community of job seekers out there, we want to help you find your way about the craze of finding the perfect career. All of our articles were not only written with expert knowledge on the topics they cover, but the resources from which we found that knowledge are listed at the bottom of each page.

In addition to these resources, our administration team is always looking to provide full on support for all your job hunting concerns. Have a question, comment, complaint, concern, or interest? Just by going to our Contact page, you can contact one of our administrators, and let us know all about it. You'll get a response via email within 12 hours upon filling out our form. We'd also love to hear your feedback on our website, so don't be afraid to bestow your thoughts of our work upon us.
